Pricing FAQ

How do you calculate project cost?

We provide a detailed estimate after initial consultation. Includes breakdown of hours, materials, and any third-party costs.

Do you offer discounts for bulk work?

Yes. Ongoing projects (10+ hours/month) and bulk services receive 10-20% discount. Contact us for custom pricing.

What about rush fees?

Expedited turnaround available. Rush fee is 25-50% of project cost depending on timeline. Discuss during consultation.

Can I change my scope mid-project?

Yes. Scope changes are documented and may increase cost. We'll notify you of any time/cost impact before proceeding.

Do you have payment plans?

For projects over $5,000, we offer payment schedules. Discuss during consultation. First 50% due to begin work.

What if I'm not satisfied?

We include unlimited revisions. If major issues exist after delivery, we address them at no cost. Refunds handled case-by-case.
