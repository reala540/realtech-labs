Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you start a project?

Most projects can start within 2-3 business days of signed agreement. For urgent work, we offer expedited onboarding. Contact us to discuss your timeline.

Do you offer NDAs and confidentiality agreements?

Yes, absolutely. Confidentiality is standard for all clients. We can sign your NDA or use our template. All client data is strictly confidential.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, credit cards, and PayPal. Most projects require 50% upfront to begin work, with final 50% due upon completion.

What if I'm not satisfied with the work?

We include multiple rounds of revisions. If major issues exist after delivery, we'll address them at no cost. Refunds are evaluated case-by-case.

How secure is my data?

We use enterprise-grade encryption for data in transit and at rest. All systems are regularly updated. Client data is never shared or stored beyond project completion.

Do you provide support after project completion?

Yes. We're available for questions, minor adjustments, and optimization advice. Support terms are included in your project agreement.
