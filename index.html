Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our services, security, and process.

How quickly can you start a project?

Most projects can start within 2-3 business days of signed agreement. For urgent work, we offer expedited onboarding. Contact us for your timeline.

Do you offer NDAs and confidentiality agreements?

Yes, absolutely. Confidentiality is standard for all clients. We can sign your NDA or use our template. All client data is treated as strictly confidential.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, credit cards, and PayPal. Most projects require 50% upfront to begin work, with final 50% due upon completion.

What if I'm not satisfied with the work?

We include multiple rounds of revisions. If major issues exist after delivery, we'll address them at no cost. Refunds are evaluated case-by-case based on work completed.

How secure is my data?

We use enterprise-grade encryption for data in transit and at rest. All systems are regularly updated with latest security patches. Client data is never shared or stored beyond project completion.

Do you provide support after project completion?

Yes. We're available for questions, minor adjustments, and optimization advice. Support terms are included in your project agreement.

Can I track project progress?

Absolutely. We provide regular updates via email, video calls, or shared dashboard depending on project scope. Communication is transparent throughout.

What's your experience with [specific technology]?

We work with most modern tech stacks. Contact us with your specific requirements and we'll let you know our experience level and recommendations.
